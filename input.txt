"I have no job, no savings. If you want to marry me, you need an annual salary of at least 500,000 yuan, otherwise I can't afford my daily expenses."

"The world is going to the dogs! Even the mighty Dark Emperor has fallen so low!" Xiao Beichen sighed.

He would not let any of those who had caused the extermination of his clan go unpunished!

Eighteen years ago, he was the eldest son of the Long family, a top-tier family in the capital!

The taller the tree, the more the wind blows.

His entire family was slaughtered, his parents even more tragically killed. Fortunately, his family guards fought their way out, saving his life. As a young boy, he wandered to Jiangzhou, where he was adopted and raised by a kind couple, taking the surname Xiao.

As he grew up, the hatred in Xiao Beichen's heart remained unquenched. He wanted to join the army on the border and learn some skills.

But, things didn't go as planned; his plane crashed on the Dark Island.

Xiao Beichen miraculously survived, even finding a way out of a desperate situation. He obtained the cultivation techniques of the Dark Island, practiced them for several years, and mastered them.

During these three years, he dared not attack his enemies.

Because, if he used even a fraction of his internal power, he would inevitably suffer a backlash from the fire poison, exploding on the spot!

"Who says I'm bragging? I'm telling the truth!"

Xiao Beichen smiled and took the tissue.

His clothes were already soaked with coffee, impossible to wipe clean.

This man was dressed in cheap clothes, probably worth less than a hundred yuan in total; he had no way of having contact with those important figures.

While the gold-digging woman's words were harsh, they were undeniably true!

Moreover, the business tycoon she spoke of was none other than the recently arrived, the renowned Ye Qingcheng!

And that…

Seemingly realizing something, the coffee shop employee didn't speak, silently turning and leaving.

Upstairs in the VIP room sat a woman whose looks, figure, and skin were absolutely flawless. Compared to her, Hu Xiaoxiao was nothing, practically a maid!

"Ms. Ye, it's time to go!"

The woman's secretary approached and said slowly.

This woman was none other than Ye Qingcheng!

The Chairwoman of the Jiangcheng Ye Group.

The renowned business tycoon.

"Mr. Ye, are you still thinking about that man?"

Secretary Wang Xiaodie curled her lip. "What kind of man could possibly make you so infatuated, Mr. Ye!"

You searched the entire city before finding one with a similar style, and every Tuesday afternoon, no matter how busy you are, you come here to sit for a while." Wang Xiaodie complained indignantly.

His ex-girlfriends should all be out there right now.

Damn it, he actually ran into her!

He'd left without saying goodbye, slept with her, and run away. If she'd found out, he'd be skinned alive!

Serves you right, you poor bastard, you bragged until you slept with a tiger's tail!
