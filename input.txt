Rain fell like knives, and night deepened.

The imperial city of the Great Zhou Dynasty collapsed in a raging fire, with black smoke billowing and soaring into the sky, making the already gloomy sky even darker.

The sound of horse hooves, shouts of killing, and wails intertwined, as if the entire city had fallen into a purgatory.

"Boom—!"

A bolt of lightning struck, illuminating a narrow alley on the east side of the imperial city.

In the alley, a dilapidated carriage was moving with difficulty, its wheels rolling over puddles, splashing muddy water.

Inside the carriage, Shen Qing clenched his fists tightly, his nails almost digging into his palms, yet there was no trace of panic on his face.

"Calm… I must be calm…"

He slowly exhaled a turbid breath, forcibly suppressing the fear in his chest that did not belong to him.

The body's instinct was trembling, weeping, and even made him want to curl up.

But he knew that once he succumbed to this emotion, he would surely die… "Your Highness, can we… can we really escape?"

In the corner of the carriage, a servant asked with a trembling voice.

Shen Qing looked up, and by the occasional flash of lightning, he clearly saw his face.

Pale, despairing, with eyes full of the fear of impending death.

He did not answer, but merely lifted a corner of the curtain and looked into the distance.

In the direction of the imperial city's main gate, flames soared into the sky, and countless iron cavalry could be vaguely seen surging in like a tide.

Those were the armies of the Xuanhuang Empire.

Just half a day ago, the Great Zhou Dynasty was completely annihilated!

And he, Shen Qing, the Tenth Prince of Great Zhou, became the Prince of a fallen nation.

Seven days ago, a large 3A game inexplicably appeared on his computer.

"No! Both sides are dead ends!"

Even at this moment of life and death, his appearance still captivated them.

At the end of the alley, several Xuanhuang soldiers could be vaguely seen searching house to house.

"Go!"
