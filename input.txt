As the first person who reached the number of kills appeared, it didn't take long for the second and third people to appear one after another.

These people even summed up the rules and came up with the easiest order to kill: children, old people, patients...

Facts have proved that the collapse of morality and order is much faster than people imagined.

Just three days later, Linshui City, a large city in Nuozhou, lost half of its population! The number of people who have achieved the number of kills has exceeded 500.

The rules say that only a maximum of 300 people can survive.

If the final number of people exceeds 300, they will be ranked from top to bottom according to the number of kills.

In order not to be overtaken by others, those who have reached the number of kills will intensify their efforts and become red-eyed.

Just like the "warm" reminder in the rules, many people began to target their relatives and friends.

When this happens, it marks the beginning of the disintegration of the family, the most basic unit of society.

"Dad, let me lend you your head."

"Son, I raised you so well, now it's time to repay me. Don't worry, I will start faster."

……

Such conversations began to appear in various families.

Are there any people who would rather die than surrender? Is there anyone who would rather die than kill someone else?

Yes, and there are many.

But unfortunately, they have been filtered out.

The rules of Yindu destined them to be eliminated.

The three rules of city battle royale are to filter out the most cruel and despicable people.

Five days later, the game comes to an end.

At this time, Linshui City was already covered in blood, with thick black smoke burning everywhere, and it looked like a scene of ruins.

Many people in later generations do not understand why a perfectly good city, once turned into a strange scene throughout the city, will turn into ruins in just a few days.

This is the truth!

There are no supernatural powers, no evil spirits running rampant, just a few rules and a human heart that has lost its restraints.

At the end of the game, there were more than a thousand people left, and without exception, they all achieved the minimum number of kills.

And they didn't stop, they continued to look for targets and kill.

The end of the city battle royale is different from the end of other weird missions.

When other weird tasks end, all players will basically gather together and wait for the task to end. At this time, most of the players were relieved. Everyone met by chance and never saw each other again after the game.

But at the end of the city battle royale, players didn't come together. Like lone wolves, they stalked the ruins of the city, watching out for others of their kind and waiting for opportunities to sneak up on them.

What they were thinking was - if they kill one more person, maybe I can survive.

As the rankings appeared, those who were not included in the rankings suddenly twisted and fell to the ground in pain.

A small blood line appeared on their waists and broke into two pieces in the middle. The broken body is still twisted, waiting for death in pain.

From beginning to end, Marshal Yindu did not show up. It didn't even appear once and caused hundreds of thousands of casualties.

This further confirmed Chen Mu's guess: Marshal Yindu was either the King of Guilty, or he was extremely close to the strength of the King of Guilty. Otherwise, it would be impossible to cause such a big storm.

Before the remaining three hundred survivors could breathe a sigh of relief, another line of bloody words appeared in front of everyone:

[Congratulations to all the winners. You are my new human servants.

There is a man named Chen Mu in Wangjiang City. If any of you can kill him, I will make him a traitor!

Once you become a treacherous general, you will enjoy endless prosperity and wealth for the rest of your life. In the strange apocalypse, there is no place where the evil generals cannot go. 】

At this point, Chen Mu finally knew why these three men in black dared to take such a big risk to assassinate him.

The temptation of the treacherous general!

Marshal Yindu actually gave them the reward of a trickster general!

Whoever can assassinate Chen Mu can become a treacherous general...

Become a scheming general...

You know, Chen Mu has trillions of ghost coins and memories of past lives.

With so many convenient bonuses, until now, Chen Mu has only reached the peak of being a cunning leader.

He is still two levels away from being a real scheming general, and there is still half a step of scheming general in between!

If it were really that easy to become a treacherous general, wouldn't it mean that in the whole weird apocalypse, there would be treacherous generals everywhere.

Marshal Yindu directly set out the conditions.

As long as it can kill Chen Mu, even if you don't even have the strength of a trickster, it can help you become a trickster general!

What a heaven-defying reward!

No wonder these three people, acting like crazy, dared to trouble Chen Mu.

Chen Mu's heart couldn't help but be filled with worry.

Can you turn an ordinary person into a treacherous general? Doesn't this mean that Marshal Yindu's strength has exceeded Chen Mu's knowledge.

You know, as a peak sly leader, Chen Mu couldn't possibly turn an ordinary person into a lower-level sly soldier all of a sudden.

Even if Marshal Yindu, as the King of Tricks, wants to turn a person into a general, it is basically impossible.

Could it be said that... Marshal Yindu's real strength has surpassed King Gui?

Thinking of this, cold sweat broke out on Chen Mu's back.

How to play this? Want me to challenge a charlatan king? It would be better to kill Chen Mu directly.

No chance of winning at all!

However, Chen Mu quickly calmed down. After thinking about it again, he discovered the problem.

If Marshal Yindu is really so defiant, then there are only two possibilities:

First, it ignores old wood. As for Chen Mu's Wangjiang City, it is really insignificant in the eyes of Weiyi who is above King Wei.

Second, it directly trampled Chen Mu to death. It is possible for such a powerful being to do it.

So the question is, why is it that Marshal Yindu is neither of these two situations? Marshal Yindu showed that he coveted Chen Mu's territory very much, and he also tried his best to let humans assassinate Chen Mu.

This is inconsistent with the strength of Marshal Yindu!

The logic is wrong!

Chen Mu has always believed that there are traces in this world. When there is a problem with logical reasoning, there must be something wrong somewhere.

Then, the most likely possibility is that Marshal Yindu is not that powerful!

Correct!

That's it.

What Marshal Yindu gave these three hundred people was just a promise, not a strange mission!

If it is a strange mission, then according to the principle of rules first, Marshal Yindu is also restricted by the rules. When someone really kills Chen Mu, Marshal Yindu must fulfill his promise.

Otherwise, Marshal Yindu will be executed by the rules for violating the rules!

Precisely because Marshal Yindu couldn't do it, it didn't dare to use weird tasks, but instead made empty promises.

This is a scam!

Obviously, those three hundred people were deceived!

What a blank wolf.

Marshal Yindu has a method. He doesn't intend to pay anything at all and just lets three hundred cannon fodder work for him.

What's more, these three hundred cannon fodder are all cruel and ruthless people.

When it came to cutting leeks, Chen Mu had to sigh: You weird and powerful ones are more ruthless.

Chen Mu said that he had learned the trick of White Wolf with Empty Hands.
