Rubbing his nose, Li Yang continued, “Isn’t my line change awesome?! I’ll learn from Zhang Baiwan and label this show a comedy! It’ll definitely be a hit!”

“Director Li really learns quickly, but… Director Zhang’s show got a lot of criticism, didn’t it…” a staff member said.

Li Yang waved his hand: "Let them scold, it's the marketing team they're criticizing, not me..."

Marketing: "???"

Just as the marketing team was internally complaining, Li Peijie slowly got up.

The next shot was about to begin.

As Li Peijie walked out of the elevator in a daze and headed downstairs, the tension he felt gradually weakened.

Li Yang frowned upon seeing this.

After a while, Li Yang, finally unable to bear it any longer, shouted at the control panel: "Cut!"

"Peijie, you're not quite there yet, it's not quite there," Li Yang said, looking at Li Peijie who had already reached the bottom of the stairs.

Li Peijie scratched his head awkwardly, he also felt the tension weakening.

But he still couldn't muster the right emotions.

"Let's do it again!" Li Yang said to everyone.

Li Peijie nodded and returned to the second shot.

But this take was even worse than the first.

The third take...

This time it was even worse!

Four takes in a row, the guy carrying the camera was panting heavily.

But Li Peijie still wasn't meeting Li Yang's expectations.

"Ugh... what am I going to do?!" Li Yang was at his wit's end.

Since Jiang Zhe stopped putting pressure on the actors, their skill level had plummeted several notches.

Li Yang stared at the control panel in front of him and fell silent.

"Director Li... I have a great idea!" Huang Bohua pointed to the sky with his right hand, looking at Li Yang.

Hearing Huang Bohua's words, Li Yang looked up, somewhat puzzled.

"Isn't Teacher Li afraid of Teacher Jiang? Let's print out Teacher Jiang's photo and put it on Teacher Li's usual route! When Teacher Li's emotions get low, we can let him see it!"

After Huang Bohua finished speaking, Li Yang slapped his thigh.

That's right! That's definitely a good idea!

Li Yang patted Huang Bohua's shoulder admiringly: "Wow! Your new brain works so well!"

With that, Li Yang arranged for a staff member to go to the print shop and print out Jiang Zhe's photo.

When the staff member returned, he looked at Li Yang with a somewhat unpleasant expression: "Director Li... the print shop... they've blacklisted us, telling us not to go there again..."

"It's alright, these few copies are enough. Go and arrange for the photos to be put up," Li Yang said.

After Li Yang finished speaking, several staff members stepped forward, just opening the A4 paper in their hands.

The next moment, the A4 paper was slammed shut again.

“Director, my stomach hurts a bit right now…”

“Director, I haven’t finished my work yet…”

“…”

In an instant, many staff members refused this easy task.

Seeing the staff around him refusing, Director Li was about to speak when Huang Bohua interrupted him: “Director, let me do it!”

“Okay! You’re the one!” Li Yang agreed immediately without hesitation.

Taking the A4 paper from a staff member, Huang Bohua jogged to the stairwell.

Slowly opening the A4 paper, Huang Bohua froze, the paper flying from his hand.

On the A4 paper, an old man wearing a Zhongshan suit, his body covered in purplish-black lividity, stared intently at something outside the paper.

What the hell is this?!

Wasn’t it supposed to be printing Teacher Jiang’s photo?

Who is this old man?!

Even through the A4 paper, Huang Bohua felt a chill rise from the soles of his feet after seeing the old man depicted.

This was a fear from the depths of his heart.

Gritting his teeth, he closed his eyes, picked up the A4 paper from the floor, casually stuck it on the wall, and quickly left the stairwell.

Reaching Li Yang's side, Huang Bohua wiped the sweat from his brow and said, "Finished, Director!"

Li Yang nodded, picked up the walkie-talkie, and shouted, "Get ready, start filming!

'Elevator,' Act 5, Shot 2, Action!"

Li Peijie was unaware that Huang Bohua had decorated the stairwell.

While Li Yang was deep in thought, Li Peijie had gone upstairs to ponder.

Pondering why he couldn't grasp that feeling.

Upon hearing Li Yang's voice, Li Peijie slowly rose, struggling to control his emotions, and walked towards the stairwell.

But as soon as he reached the stairwell door, Li Peijie felt a sense of powerlessness.

It was a feeling of helplessness at his own inadequacy as an actor!

He mentally counted the stairs, knowing he had to look up after three more steps to portray the first time he experienced a hallucination.

One step...

Two steps...

Three steps...

Upon reaching the third step, Li Peijie slowly looked up.
